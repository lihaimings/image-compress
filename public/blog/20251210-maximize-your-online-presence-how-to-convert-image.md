## Understanding WebP Format

### What is WebP?

WebP is an open-source image format developed by Google in 2010. It offers better compression and faster loading times compared to traditional formats like JPEG and PNG, making it a popular choice for modern web design.

### Key Features of WebP

- **High Compression:** WebP reduces file size up to 25% more than JPEG and PNG.
- **Speed:** Faster image loading can lead to improved user experience and SEO rankings.
- **Lossless and Lossy Compression:** Supports both lossless (no quality loss) and lossy compression (some quality loss).
